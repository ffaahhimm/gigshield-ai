# 🚀 GigShield AI – Adaptive Income Protection for Delivery Workers

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

India’s gig delivery workers (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) often lose income due to uncontrollable external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, pollution, floods, or curfews. These events directly reduce their working hours and earnings.

Currently, there is **no financial safety net** to protect their income during such disruptions.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**GigShield AI** is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect delivery workers from income loss.

Our system:

* Predicts disruptions before they occur
* Automatically activates insurance coverage
* Verifies worker activity and location
* Detects fraud using intelligent models
* Instantly processes payouts

This ensures **fast, fair, and automated income protection**.

---

## 🎯 Unique Features (What Makes Us Stand Out)

* 🔮 **Predictive AI Engine** – Forecasts disruptions before they happen
* 💰 **Dynamic Weekly Premium** – Adjusts pricing based on risk
* 📊 **Smart Payout Calculation** – Based on actual income loss
* 🛡 **Fraud Detection System** – GPS + activity + trust score validation
* 🗺 **Geo-Risk Heatmap** – Visual map of high-risk delivery zones
* ⚡ **Zero-Touch Claims** – Fully automated claim processing

---

## 👤 Target Persona

Food Delivery Workers (Zomato / Swiggy)

---

## ⚙️ Workflow

1. Worker registers on the platform
2. AI analyzes location risk and calculates weekly premium
3. Worker purchases insurance policy
4. System continuously monitors external disruptions (weather, pollution, etc.)
5. AI predicts or detects disruption events
6. Worker activity and location are verified
7. Claim is automatically triggered
8. Instant payout is processed

---

## 📊 Weekly Premium Model

| Risk Level  | Weekly Premium |
| ----------- | -------------- |
| Low Risk    | ₹15/week       |
| Medium Risk | ₹25/week       |
| High Risk   | ₹40/week       |

Premium is dynamically calculated using AI based on location and historical risk data.

---

## 🌦 Parametric Triggers

| Trigger      | Condition                |
| ------------ | ------------------------ |
| Heavy Rain   | Rainfall > 50mm          |
| Extreme Heat | Temperature > 45°C       |
| Pollution    | AQI > 400                |
| Flood Alert  | Government flood warning |
| Curfew       | Movement restrictions    |

When these conditions are met → **automatic claim activation**

---

## 🛡 Fraud Prevention Mechanisms

* Worker must be active before disruption
* Worker must be present in affected zone
* Minimum working time required
* Trust Score system for reliability

### Trust Score System

| Score | Action                 |
| ----- | ---------------------- |
| 80+   | Instant claim approval |
| 50–80 | Normal verification    |
| <50   | Manual review          |

---

## 💸 Smart Payout Calculation

Instead of fixed payout, we calculate income loss:

Example:

* Deliveries/hour = 4
* Earnings/delivery = ₹50
* Disruption duration = 3 hours

Payout:

₹50 × 4 × 3 = ₹600
